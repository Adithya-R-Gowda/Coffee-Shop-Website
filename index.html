About Us

What Makes Our Coffee Special?

Big Cup uses only the ripest and selectively-handpicked coffee cherries from the estates of Sandalkad, Sandalwood and Old Kent. The Bota family manages the Sandalkad & Sandalwood estates, while Old Kent is managed by the Thaikappa family.

The coffee is grown after careful assessment, choosing only the highest grade of Arabica and Robusta varieties. The Coffee bushes are grown at an altitude of over 1000 masl under the two-tier Canopy of rainforest trees, making it bird-friendly and 100% shade-grown. The coffee cherries are closely monitored all the way until it's roasted in house and brewed to perfection. Each process is special - the journey of coffee beans from harvesting, drying, curing & hulling until it's roasted, brewed and finally relished by our patrons. This very journey imparts - flavours, aromas and personality, uniquely expressed by the "Terroir", as manifested in the cup, bringing you the essence, history and vision of the Bota and the Thaikappa family. Our families have owned and managed coffee plantations for more than 150 years and Big Cup is the fusion of the expertise in farming and hospitality.
